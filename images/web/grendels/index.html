Creature Comforts (Organic)

380 different species of migratory birds, small mammals and insects live comfortably in and below the shade tree canopy that grows over the coffee plantations of El Salvador. Then we add some WONDERMUCK! and a little Sumatran, balancing smoke and body.

Jonny Tsunami

Our newest espresso blend. Backing off on the darkness to allow for a more complex shot of espresso, Jonny Tsunami is a blend of African, Indonesian and South American Coffees. Made almost entirely with direct trade beans, it has a smooth, rich buttery flavor, and a good, full body. Works beautifully as an espresso or a french press.

THUNDERMUCK!

DARK • FULL BODY • MEDIUM ACIDITY •

A complex blend of coffees from Central American, South American and Indonesia. Roasted to the edge of darkness to give it a rich, smooth, smokey and balanced flavor. Full-Bodied with a Medium Acidity.

El Salvador

Medium (Direct Trade, Shade Grown)

Grown at a high elevation in volcanic soil. Shade grown, Medium Roast. Sweet and floral flavor with a caramel smooth finish. We buy this coffee directly from the Menendez family who grows it. They have been growing coffee in the Apaneca-Llamatepec Mountains in El Salvador for 4 generations.
